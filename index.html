Who We Are

We don't just
open doors —
we move the walls

At Go Outside Foundation, we believe exploration isn't a luxury — it's a right. Every young person, regardless of zip code, deserves access to the experiences that unlock ambition, empathy, and confidence that only comes from seeing the world with fresh eyes.

Our leadership team and board bring experience, passion, and dedication to ensuring GOF remains a trusted partner for schools, parents, and community leaders across Chicago and beyond.

What We Do

Programs built
to move youth
forward

Our programs are designed to inspire youth through hands-on experiences that broaden perspectives, strengthen character, and prepare the next generation for leadership in their communities and beyond. Together, these initiatives foster well-rounded development and global awareness.

01
🏫
The W.A.Y. Club
World Around You Club — on-site experiences at schools with study support, panel discussions, and cultural learning that connects youth to the broader world from where they stand.
→
02
✈️
Go Far
Off-site trips introducing youth to local and national travel, tourism, and wellness. From day excursions to multi-day journeys, Go Far expands what feels possible.
→
03
🏃
Go Outside Moves
Our health and wellness program promoting fitness, mental health, and character building — because strong leaders begin with a strong foundation in all three.
→
04
🤝
Go Outside Gives
Volunteer and charity initiatives that teach youth the value of service. Through giving, young people discover their own power to transform the communities around them.
→
